Introducing Spelling and Syntax Doctor

Get rid of the troublesome spelling and syntax errors from your code.

About

S & S Doctor is an add-on that works with any text editor.


If you use Sublime Text, Text Wrangler, any other editor or even Notepad to write your code, simply activate it in S & S Doctor and it will automatically check your spelling and syntax for errors as you type.


You won't have to stop working because you just found that something is not working and have to look for the error - S & S Doctor will highlight it for you.
